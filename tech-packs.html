Why Tech Packs Matter

A Tech Pack is the essential blueprint for your garment. It acts as the primary communication tool between the designer and the factory, ensuring that your vision is translated into physical form without ambiguity.

Prevent Factory Errors

By defining every measurement, fabric weight, and stitch type, we eliminate the guesswork that leads to costly sampling errors.

Structured Scaling

Structured documentation allows you to move production between factories seamlessly while maintaining consistent quality.
